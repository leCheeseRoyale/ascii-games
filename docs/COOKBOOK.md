# Cookbook

Copy-pasteable recipes, split by topic. Imports use `@engine` / `@game` / `@ui` / `@shared` aliases.

## [defineGame Recipes](cookbook/define-game.md)

Declarative, turn-based games with `defineGame`. Multiplayer in one line with `createMultiplayerGame`.

## [ECS Patterns](cookbook/ecs-patterns.md)

Scenes, systems with priority, entity factories, safe destruction, entity pools.

## [Rendering & Art Assets](cookbook/rendering-and-art.md)

Styled text tags, mixed-font HUD rows, text blocks, text effects, camera, art assets, sprite caching, colorMap, static vs interactive rendering.

## [Game Feel & Input](cookbook/game-feel.md)

Screen flash, blinking, knockback, slow motion, trails, `onCollide`, quick HUD. Keyboard, mouse, touch gestures, virtual controls, gamepad, remappable bindings.

## [Gameplay Systems](cookbook/gameplay.md)

Inventory, equipment, currency, crafting, loot tables, quests, achievements, damage, AI behaviors, wave spawner, spatial hash, turn-based phases, procgen (dungeon, BSP, cave, noise), enemy AI patterns.

## [Text-Aware Physics](cookbook/text-physics.md)

Auto-colliders, spring presets, `spawnText` / `spawnSprite`, cursor repulsion, ambient drift, text measurement helpers. Complete interactive title screen recipe.

## [UI & Infrastructure](cookbook/ui-and-infrastructure.md)

Dialog, choice, menus, scroll panels, grids, tooltips, tabs, canvas UI. Mobile touch, viewport. Multiplayer adapters, TurnSync. Persistence, save slots, compression, leaderboards. Scene transitions, pause menu.
